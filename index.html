The Torchbearer of Inclusive India

Rajeev Bhatt is one of India’s most respected pioneers in the field of inclusive education and learning disabilities. With a journey spanning over three decades, he has dedicated his life to ensuring that children with autism, dyslexia, ADHD, and other learning differences are not defined by limitations—but empowered by opportunity.

Honoured with the National Award for Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities, presented by the Hon’ble President of India Droupadi Murmu, Rajeev Bhatt’s work represents a transformational shift in how India understands neurodiversity, education, and dignity.
